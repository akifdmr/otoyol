My account

Your account at www.vinetki.bg gives you access to additional functionalities:

  • - checking the validity of the e-Vignette;
  • - e-mail notifications about the expiry date of e-Vignette of your vehicle;
  • - entering and saving of data for several vehicles;
  • - easily purchase a new eVignette for the vehicles saved in your account;
  • - your purchases history;
  • - the ability to download information about your purchases;
  • - receiving commercial messages with special offers by ITS AD and partners;

How can I recover my password?

All you have to do is enter your email address in the first step when logging into account where there is a "Forgot password" box by which will automatically receive an email with a new password, through which you can restore access to your account.
